Sage Barista Express

All-in-one with integrated conical burr grinder. Dose, grind, tamp, and extract from one device.

Usage tips:

  • Preheat your portafilter before pulling a shot.
  • Use 18–20g for a double espresso.

Hario V60 Dripper

The classic conical dripper beloved by specialty coffee professionals worldwide. Ceramic edition for superior heat retention.

Chemex 6-Cup Classic

An icon of industrial design from 1941. Brews a clean, clear, and exceptionally bright cup.

Usage tips:

  • Use a medium-coarse grind (similar to sea salt).
  • Total brew time should be 4–5 minutes.
  • Coffee stays warm in the Chemex for up to an hour.

Bodum Brazil French Press

Timeless, effortless, and wonderfully forgiving. Makes a rich, full-bodied coffee with wonderful texture.

Usage tips:

  • Use a coarse grind to prevent over-extraction.
  • Steep for exactly 4 minutes before pressing.
  • Pour immediately after pressing.

Grinder

Baratza Encore Burr Grinder

Entry-level burr grinder widely regarded as the best value-for-money on the market. 40 grind settings.

Usage tips:

  • Clean the burrs every 2–3 weeks.
  • Settings 8–12 suit most pour-over methods.
  • Grind only what you need — freshness is key.

£135.00

Stovetop

Bialetti Moka Express (6-Cup)

The Italian original since 1933. Makes a strong, rich coffee somewhere between espresso and drip.

Usage tips:

  • Fill the base with pre-boiled water.
  • Use medium-fine grind — finer than filter.
  • Never pack grounds tightly.

£32.00

Cold Brew

OXO Cold Brew Maker (32oz)

Steep, permeate, and serve with this elegant, drip-free system. Makes smooth, low-acid cold brew concentrate in 12–24 hours.

Usage tips:

  • Use a coarse grind at 1:4 ratio (coffee to water).
  • Steep 12–24 hours in the fridge.
  • Dilute concentrate 1:1 with cold water to serve.

£49.99
